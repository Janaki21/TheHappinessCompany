WHAT IS DOPAMINE?.

Dopamine is a type of neurotransmitter, which is a chemical messenger that transmits signals in the brain and other areas of the nervous system. It plays a crucial role in various functions, including movement, motivation, reward, and the regulation of mood. Dopamine is produced in several areas of the brain, particularly in the substantia nigra and the ventral tegmental area
